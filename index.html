Luxury vehicles at your fingertips.

CharterAutos is the leader in luxury car hire & chauffeur driven services. Our services are available in the two major cities of Johannesburg and Cape Town and cater for the discerning client.

The extensive fleet of CharterAutos consists of Super Cars, Exotics, Sports Cars, Convertibles, Hardtops, Sedans, SUV’s, 4×4’s, Passenger / People Carriers and Luxurious Limousines. Our car hire rental services include: Armoured Vehicles, Chauffeur Driven Car Hire, Executive Car Hire, Film Shoots, Wedding Car Hire, VIP Protection Security Services, Promotional Vehicles, Point to Point Transfers, Matric Dance Car Hire and Limousine Hire
